Urban Survival

Being a martial artist also means being able to protect oneself and others from harm. This is more so in an urban setting, where dangers may lie in every street corner and dark alley.

The discipline and awareness you hone will ensure that no training goes to waste, and respect for others in the Dojo translates into respect for all persons as individuals with dignity and grace.

...but woe to the person who thinks evil to others!
