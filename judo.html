Judo

The beautiful gentle art...of destruction

Judo was created in 1882 by Kano Jigoro Shihan. As an educational method derived from the ancient Japanese Samurai art of Jujutsu, Judo became an official Olympic sport in 1964. Judo is a highly codified sport in which the mind controls the expression of the body and is a sport which contributes to educating & "in-forming" individuals.

Beyond competitions and combat, Judo involves technical research, practice of katas, self-defense work, physical preparation and sharpening of spirit.

As a discipline derived from ancestral traditions, judo was designed by Shihan Kano to embrace the strengths of a pedagogical education system.
